FAQs

Does this tool reduce image quality?
No. Processing runs at the image‚Äôs original resolution and only changes the transparency of background pixels. Subject colors and details are preserved.

Which formats are supported?
You can upload JPG, PNG, and WebP. The result is saved as a transparent PNG for wide compatibility with editors and design tools.

Why do I see a checkerboard pattern?
The checkerboard shows transparency. If you see the grid, it means those areas are fully or partially transparent, not gray or colored.

What settings should I start with?
Begin with a Tolerance around 40 and a Strength around 18. Increase Tolerance if background pixels remain; increase Strength slightly to soften edges if they look harsh.

Are my images uploaded?
Local processing happens in your browser, keeping your files private while you work. If you enable API mode, the image is sent to the configured service for complex scenes.

Can I process multiple images at once?
This demo focuses on single images for speed and simplicity. For batches, repeat the steps or integrate an API workflow on your server.

What is the maximum image size?
Very large images can slow down the browser. For best performance, keep files under ~25 MB and use modern hardware.

The download shows a checkerboard ‚Äî is that saved?
The checkerboard is only a preview of transparency. The saved PNG has transparent pixels; most editors replace them with their own background.

Can I export as JPG?
JPG does not support transparency. If you need JPG, place the PNG on a solid background in your editor and then export as JPG.

Some background remains ‚Äî what should I try?
Increase Tolerance in small steps (5‚Äì10). If the subject edge looks thin after that, reduce Tolerance slightly and increase Strength a little.

Edges look jagged ‚Äî how do I smooth them?
Increase Strength to softly feather the transition. Also ensure your source photo is sharp and evenly lit for cleaner masks.

I see a faint color halo around the subject ‚Äî how do I fix it?
Nudge Tolerance down slightly and raise Strength to blend the edge, or re‚Äëshoot with stronger contrast between subject and background.

Does it work on mobile?
Yes, modern mobile browsers are supported. Very large images may process more slowly due to memory limits.

Will EXIF data be preserved?
The transparent PNG is newly generated and typically does not include the original EXIF metadata.

Can I undo changes and start over?
Use the Clear button to reset the canvas and results, then upload the image again with updated settings.
