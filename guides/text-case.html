Convert text to UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, Sentence case, or snake_case instantly.

What is it?

A formatter that normalizes text casing and cleans spacing/punctuation.

Why use it?

  • Consistent style in emails, posts, and docs.
  • Prepare SEO titles and slugs.
  • Fix messy pasted text.

How to use

  1. Open Text Case Converter.
  2. Paste/type text; choose a case style.
  3. Copy result with one click.

Tips

  • Use Title Case for headlines (minor words often lowercase).
  • Sentence case is best for readability.
