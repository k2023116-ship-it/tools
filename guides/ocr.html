OCR converts text in images (photos, scans, screenshots) into editable, searchable text ‚Äî directly in your browser.

What is it?

Optical Character Recognition detects and extracts printed or handwritten text from images and produces selectable text output.

Why use it?

  • Copy text from scans/screenshots without retyping.
  • Digitize notes, books, bills, and forms.
  • Make content searchable and accessible.

How to use

  1. Open the tool: Image to Text.
  2. Upload a JPG/PNG or drop a screenshot.
  3. Click Extract Text to process.
  4. Copy the output or download as a text file.

Tips

  • Use clear images with good contrast for better accuracy.
  • Cropping out borders or noise can improve results.
