What is it?

A lightweight PDF composer that merges multiple files into a single, shareable PDF.

Why use it?

  • Prepare assignments, invoices, or presentations quickly.
  • Ensure consistent pages for printing/sharing.
  • No watermark, no upload limits.

How to use

  1. Go to PDF Maker.
  2. Add images/docs, arrange order by drag-and-drop.
  3. Choose page size (A4/Letter), margins, and orientation.
  4. Export and download the PDF.

Tips

  • Use WebP/PNG for crisp graphics.
  • Keep consistent orientation for better readability.
