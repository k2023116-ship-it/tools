About Image Text Extractor

Toolify’s Image Text Extractor is a free OCR utility powered by Tesseract.js. It lets you copy or download text instantly from scanned pages, screenshots, or photos — all within your browser. No server uploads, ensuring privacy.

Key Features

  • Extracts text from JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, and other formats.
  • Supports multiple languages like English, French, Arabic, Hindi, Urdu, etc.
  • Works fully offline after the first load (thanks to Tesseract.js WASM).
  • Copy or download results instantly as .txt.
  • Optimized preprocessing: grayscale + contrast for better OCR accuracy.

How to Use

  1. Upload, drop, or paste an image into the tool.
  2. Choose OCR language from the dropdown.
  3. Click Extract Text to process.
  4. Copy or download the recognized text instantly.

FAQs

Does this tool work offline? ✅ Yes, once the model loads.

Is my image uploaded to a server? ❌ No, everything stays on your device.

Which languages are supported? English, Arabic, Hindi, Urdu, French, Spanish, and more.

Best results tip? Use sharp, high-contrast images with clear text.
