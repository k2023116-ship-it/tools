About Word & Character Counter

Toolify's Word & Character Counter is a free online utility that helps writers, students, bloggers, and professionals instantly count words, characters, spaces, and empty lines. Whether you're preparing an essay, social media post, or coding file, this tool ensures accuracy and saves time. Perfect for combining with our Text Case Converter and Text Summarizer.

How to Count Words & Characters

  1. Type or paste your text in the input box.
  2. Upload a TXT, DOCX, or other supported file.
  3. Drag & drop files into the drop zone for quick access.
  4. View live counts of words, characters, spaces, and empty lines.
  5. Download the processed text as a TXT file.

Word Counter Features

  • Instant word and character counting.
  • Counts spaces and empty lines for accurate formatting checks.
  • Drag & drop file upload with DOCX support.
  • Download results in plain text format.
  • Works directly in your browser, no installation needed.
